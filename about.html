Welcome to the Digital World Academy

The Digital World Academy is a platform dedicated to finding mentors in a set of essential areas in the digital economy. For this, we count with a personalized tracing method of student in which it advances after to approve, at least, the 80% of evaluated content.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is a reality in which the people take initiative to forming and growing for themselves without to depend to traditional institutions like universities and states, through sharing knowledge with experimented professionals in various labor fields.

Methodology

Our methodology is based on a personalized advisory of the student, for that achieving to learn, from 0, all the competencies of labor field of your interest. To make it, obligatorily must dominate, at least, the 80% of evaluated content before to continue to the next level. That assures a complete preparation focus in a formation that value quality over quantity. This implies to form less people, though a higher preparation level.

Mentors

Our mentors are selected for their projects and not for their resumes. This implies that stand out more for their experience and less for their titles or courses. At the same time, they try to use a practice teaching system based in challenges to achieve. The theory as well is taken in consideration and it develops through a personalized conversation between mentor and student until to achieve compression and not memorization of the contents.
